Privacy Policy

This game respects your privacy. The app does not require account creation and can be played offline. Certain data is collected automatically to support gameplay features, advertising, and improve app stability.

Information We Collect

The app may collect the following types of data:

  • Gameplay data, such as completed levels, scores, and leaderboard entries
  • User-provided name, such as a nickname generated by the player for leaderboards
  • App usage data, including in-game events and interactions
  • Crash and diagnostic data, such as crash logs and device information
  • Device or installation identifiers, generated automatically to associate gameplay data with a device or app instance
  • Advertising identifiers, such as the Google Advertising ID, used to support in-app advertising
  • Device information, including device model, OS version, language settings, and general location (country or region level)

Advertising

The game displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These services may automatically collect and use certain information to deliver and measure ads, improve ad relevance, prevent fraud, and provide aggregated analytics about ad performance.

Users can reset or limit the use of their advertising identifier through their device settings.

Optional Online Features

The game includes optional online features such as global leaderboards. Participation in these features is optional and not required to play the game.

The game may also offer optional sign-in using Google Play Games Services. If you choose to sign in, Google may provide a player identifier and display name, which may be used for leaderboards and achievements.

How We Use the Data

  • To store and restore game progress
  • To display leaderboard rankings
  • To analyze gameplay behavior and improve game balance
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To deliver and measure advertisements

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. However, certain data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers that process data on our behalf to support app functionality, analytics, and advertising services.

These providers may include:

  • Analytics and crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Game platform services for leaderboards and achievements

Data Security

Data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit using industry-standard security measures.
